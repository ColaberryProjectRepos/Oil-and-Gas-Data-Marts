  1. Use of Latest Technology: In the business world today, data is one of the most crucial assets for any business as most companies strive to gain a competitive edge. In order for oil and gas companies to cut costs, boost efficiency and build their reputation, they must leverage the latest technology such as using cloud-based platforms to host their business applications, adopting IoT devices and edge computing to take advantage of sophisticated sensors and for better connectivity, use asset management software to improve management of resources and reduce risk and use the smart oilfield technology to help automate processes.
  2. Data Analytics: Oil and gas companies need to leverage Data Analytics to use data and have insight into the state of the market at all times. This will help them obtain real-time data on the performance of their equipment and all systems in their organization, help managers make quick and fast business decisions and help the companies to comply with stringent regulations in the industry.
